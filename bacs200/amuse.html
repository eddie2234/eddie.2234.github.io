This page contains some of the things I find amusing, and it so happens to be about the different brands of clothes that I prefer to buy.

Hollister

Hollister actually happens to be one of the first brands that I have been rocking with since High School. I like the unique designs and colors that they have to offer regarding shirts. They also have ripped jeans, which is probably my favorite type of jeans to wear.

Hollister

American Eagle

American Eagle is also one of my favorite brands when it comes to shopping for clothes. I usually end up buying more button-up shirts and flannels at American Eagle. I also tend to buy more casual pants here as the pants tend to fit more comfortably.

American Eagle

Nike

Nike is also a favorite brand of mine, but I usually shop for shoes when I'm at Nike. There are times where I find some cool sweaters or sweatpants that I occasionally get.
