Vision

Hav A-Sol is a non-profit organization that believes in impacting the lives of those who are most at-risk with a quality pair of shoes. Hav A Sol's goal is to restore confidence through impact, encourage a healthier lifestyle, and help prevent diseases. Hav A-Sol also believes in building a sense of community.

Strategies

Three Strategies

  • Developing new partnerships to receive donations for a good cause.
  • Allowing recipients to choose for themselves.
  • Providing a platform for those seeking to donate or part-take in the movement.
