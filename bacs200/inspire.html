Will Smith

In my eyes a hero is someone who inspires you to do push yourself. A hero has values and morals in which we seek to follow. Therefore, I really had to think about who is one of my heroes.

The heroes that I admire have the following characteristics.

  • Leadership - always ready to take on a challenge
  • Humbleness - someone who has a good work ethic and focused on their own future
  • Visionary - someone who can envision the life they desire and turning it into reality
  • Ambitious - never satisfied always wanting more for themselves

As I mentioned earlier, I really had to think about someone who met my characteristics of a hero. When I sat down an thought about it for a second the first person that came to mind was Will Smith. He is someone who I inspire to be not only as a human being, but as a husband and as a father one day. He has showned me that we often times we are going to fail at life, but with failure comes valuable life lessons.
